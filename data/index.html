Thanks for installing Grin!

Grin is a Firefox addon that enables you to support a charity of your choice at no aditional cost to you using the Amazon Smile program. Grin will ensure that every purchase you make through Amazon will result in a small donation.

The next time you log in to Amazon you'll be asked to choose a charity. Please consider setting the Mozilla Foundation as your charity of choice.
